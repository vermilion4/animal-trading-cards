Siberian Husky

Despite Their Appearance, Siberian Huskies Aren't "Part Wolf"

  • Height: 20 to 23 inches tall
  • Life Span: 12 to 15 years
  • Weight: 35 to 60 pounds
  • Dog Breed Group: Working Dogs

A Siberian husky is an unmistakable breed. Powerfully built and compact, the combination of their thick coat and piercing eyes marks them out among other dog breeds, and even among other high-latitude dog breeds. They look like noble savages, ready to take on the wild wastes they were bred to handle alongside their human masters in the early years of arctic civilization.

Snow Leopard

Snow Leopards are High Altitude Acrobats

  • Height: 2.1 meters tall
  • Life Span: 10 to 12 years
  • Weight: 50 to 90 pounds
  • Habitat: harsh, snowy, rugged alpine regions

Snow leopards are around 75-150cm from head to the base of the tail, with the tail adding on another 80-105cm, which makes them smaller than the other big cats. Snow leopards generally weigh between 25-55kg, although some large males have been recorded weighing up to 75kg.
